ABOUT ELTEK LABS

Your Single-Source Partner for Electrical Insulation Testing

ELTEK Labs' customer base spans companies large and small, domestic and international — from manufacturers of individual materials to builders of complex machinery. We work collaboratively to acquire the data needed to make technically sound decisions.

WHAT WE DO

Full-Spectrum Testing Capabilities

Electrical Testing

Up to 150kV AC / 100kV DC. Dissipation factor, dielectric breakdown, insulation resistance, impulse testing, voltage endurance, and surface tracking.

Thermal Exposures

100+ laboratory ovens up to 350°C. Cold chambers down to -30°C. Three oven sizes for wide-ranging test object dimensions.

Compatibility Testing

CCT / Sealed Tube testing. Immersion in oils, water, refrigerants, gases and vapor. More CCT testing performed than all other labs worldwide combined.

Flammability Testing

Open flame (vertical, horizontal, needle) and non-flame sources (hot wire, glow wire). Tested per ASTM, IEC, ISO, NEMA, and UL standards.

Physical Properties

Tensile, flexural, compressive, and impact strength. Deformation under load, water absorption, and hardness testing per ASTM and ISO standards.

Humidity & Condensation

Five chambers capable of up to 100% RH with visible condensation. Interior dimensions up to 1500mm wide for large test objects.

ACCREDITATION & STANDARDS

ISO/IEC 17025 Quality Management

ELTEK Labs complies with ISO/IEC 17025 as the foundation of our Quality Management System and laboratory operations. Our facility is accredited by the International Accreditation Service (IAS) as Testing Laboratory TL-457.

ELTEK is a Third Party Testing Laboratory for internationally recognized Certifying Bodies and remains an active participant on technical committees developing new requirements for safety and performance.
